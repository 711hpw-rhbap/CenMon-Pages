Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Tuesday February 25, 2025

1. Introduction

CenMon is an application developed by Air Force Research Lab 711 Human Performance Wing RHBAP. The app connects to ZOLL XSeries medical monitoring devices to monitor and display real-time vitals from multiple devices simultaneously.

2. Data Collection

CenMon only collects and stores data streamed from ZOLL XSeries devices and any patient records manually created by the user. The app does not collect any personal data about the user or their device.

3. Sensitive Information

The app does not allow users to associate actual patient PII (Personally Identifiable Information) with vitals collected from medical monitoring devices. This ensures no sensitive information is stored on the device.

4. How Data is Collected

CenMon streams vitals data directly from ZOLL XSeries devices and stores it locally on the user's device. The data is never transmitted to an internet-hosted server.

5. Purpose of Data Collection

The app collects data to display current vitals and historical charts for medical monitoring purposes.

6. Data Sharing

CenMon does not share any user or collected data with third parties.

7. Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used in CenMon.

8. Data Storage & Security

All collected data is stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

9. Data Retention

Data is retained on the device until the user chooses to delete it.

10. User Rights & Control

Users have full control over their data and can delete any or all data directly from the app.
