Getting Started

CenMon connects to ZOLL XSeries medical monitoring devices to stream and display real-time vitals. If you're new to the app, follow these steps:

  • Ensure your ZOLL XSeries device is powered on and configured for network communication.
  • Connect your iPhone or iPad to the same network as the medical device.
  • Launch CenMon and setup a monitor by selecting a device and patient to begin monitoring.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Does CenMon store or transmit patient data?

No. CenMon only stores vitals data locally on your device and does not transmit any patient data to external servers.

2. How can I delete recorded data?

Data can be deleted directly by deleting a patient record.

3. Is CenMon compatible with other medical devices?

Currently, CenMon is designed specifically for ZOLL XSeries monitors. Support for additional devices may be added in the future.
